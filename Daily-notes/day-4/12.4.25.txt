Division: Division is used to divide the whole web page into different blocks. 
In otherwise, used to group set of html elements into one unit.

    Syntax:

        <div>

            Children elements
        
        </div>

Background Image: Displays image as a background.

    Syntax:

        background-image: url(imageName.ext);

    Note: Background Image height and width will depend on foreground content size.

Background Size: Specifies the size of the background.

    Syntax:

        background-size: 100px 200px | 10% 100% | cover;

        cover - It covers whole foreground content size.

Background Position: Specifies the position of the background.

    Syntax:

        background-position: top left | top center | top right | bottom left | bottom center | bottom right | center center;

Background Repeat: Allows us to control the repeation of background.

    Syntax:

        background-repeat: no-repeat | repeat | repeat-x | repeat-y;
